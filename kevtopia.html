Welcome to Kevtopia

_______________________________________

Kevtopia was a small random creative world at first, which then grew in the huge city it is today!

How it began

One day, I was bored and felt creative. I loaded up a new creative Minecraft world and started building a house with a friend of mine. Then we built a skyscraper to test out our skills. It didn't really look good. This skyscraper is now known as the Kev Cup Secondary Office. Then I got help from some more friends and we then starting constructing more skyscrapers and houses. Before you know it, Kevtopia became a thing!

Old Kevtopia

You may know Kevtopia as a super futuristic metro city, but do you know how it was before?

Abandoned Kevtopia

Kevtopia used be a super small community with about 8 small buildings. This is still reachable, close to Hank & Frank's Industries skyscrapers. It is now an island that is filled with overgrown trees. It may even be haunted!

Q & A

What is Kev Cup?

Kev Cup is a mega manufacturing company that produces lots of things, mostly computer components. We also makes cups as merch. (In Minecraft)

How do I play this map?

There will be a public Minecraft server for both versions of Minecraft when all cities are built. (This won't happen in a loooooooooooooong time)

What is Kevtopia based off of?

Kevtopia is based off of various cities such as New York City, Dubai, Taipei, and Los Angeles
